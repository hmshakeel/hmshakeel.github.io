About Me

I am a researcher in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, with expertise in Energy Informatics and Healthcare applications. I completed my PhD at the University of Huddersfield, where I developed AI-driven predictive models and visual analytics tools for energy efficiency in residential buildings. Currently, I work as a Research Officer in AI at Leeds Beckett University.
