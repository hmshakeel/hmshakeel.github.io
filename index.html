About Me

I am a dedicated researcher and lecturer specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science. My research focuses on developing AI-driven solutions for energy efficiency, healthcare diagnostics, and explainable AI systems. With a strong academic background and professional experience in both industry and academia, I am passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems.

My PhD research at the University of Huddersfield explored AI-driven predictive analytics and interactive visualization to enhance energy efficiency in residential buildings, for which I was awarded the Smart House Scholarship.
