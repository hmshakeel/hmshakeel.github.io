AI-supported ADHD referral and stratification workflow

Developed a digital data-gathering and stratification workflow for ADHD referrals with clinicians at South West Yorkshire Partnership NHS Foundation Trust. The workflow is live within the Trust's adult ADHD service and supports structured referral assessment, interpretable analysis and service-level evaluation.

What makes the work deployable in real settings.

Applied AI projects need more than model accuracy. They need governance, reproducibility, interpretation and a workflow that matches practitioner needs.

Data strategy Data-management planning, quality checks, feature engineering and reproducible pipelines.
Validation Model comparison, clinical relevance, sensitivity checks and interpretable reporting.
Governance Ethical awareness, NHS-linked collaboration and appropriate research governance frameworks.
Usability Stakeholder-facing outputs, decision-support views and communication for non-technical users.
